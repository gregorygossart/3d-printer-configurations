## Notes
- Printing miniatures with very small support bits (sub-1mm).
- Cooling is correctly disabled for the first layers, but when the fan kicks in it goes immediately to full speed.
- These tiny support structures get swept away by the sudden blast of air at full fan speed.
- Need incremental/gradual fan speed ramp-up after the no-cooling layers, to avoid blowing off small support bits before they have time to solidify and adhere.
