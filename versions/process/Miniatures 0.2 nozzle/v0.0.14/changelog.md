# Changelog (v0.0.13 -> v0.0.14)

## Process: Miniatures 0.2 nozzle

*   **Optimized Surface Quality**
    *   **Uniform 0.2mm Line Width**:
        *   Changed all `Line width` settings (Default, First layer, Walls, Top surface, Infill, Support) to `0.2 mm`. This matches the nozzle diameter exactly, leading to a "softer" and more consistent surface finish as validated by the paper test.

## Notes
* Version 0.0.14 builds upon the solid stability of v0.0.13 but shifts focus towards surface quality based on the paper test results.
* The matching of line width to nozzle diameter (0.2mm) has proven to provide a better surface texture.
