## Notes

- **Support-to-Bed Adhesion**: Solid (no failure there).
- **Incident**: The miniature base (mostly resting on supports, not the bed) detached from its supports and was sent flying.
- **Cause Analysis**: At 45 degrees, the model is highly leveraged. A high-speed (600mm/s) travel collision likely snapped the miniature off its support interfaces.
- **Environmental**: Auxiliary fan (35%) may have contributed to warping on the tilted base, increasing collision risk.
- **Result**: Spaghetti on the left side, second miniature impacted by the flying debris.
