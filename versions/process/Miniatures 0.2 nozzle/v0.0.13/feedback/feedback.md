## Observations
- **Visual Quality**: 8/10 (Global quality is good, but overhangs are poor)
- **Dimensional Accuracy**: Good
- **Strength/Durability**: Small parts (bow string, arrow tip) are fragile during support removal.
- **Issues Encountered**:
    - **Support Engulfment**: Small parts like the bow were completely engulfed, leading to breakage during removal.
    - **Difficult Removal**: Supports in tight spaces (between torso, legs, and weapon) are hard to reach and remove.
    - **Overhang Quality**: Bottom surfaces/overhangs are "ugly" with visible filament strings.
    - **Base Vibration**: The base trembled during printing, though it didn't fail.

## Notes
- The support stability is excellent; it didn't move at all.
- Dynamic poses worked well.
- Need to focus on reducing "engulfment" of fine details.
- Need to improve overhang/bottom surface quality.
- The base trembling suggests we might need more stability for the model itself if it's tall and thin, or slower speeds for those regions.
