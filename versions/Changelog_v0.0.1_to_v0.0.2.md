# Changelog (v0.0.1 -> v0.0.2)

## Filament: Sunlu PLA+ Grey
* **Cooling**
    * **Auxiliary part cooling fan**: Changed `Fan speed` from `10%` to `0%`.

## Process: Miniatures 0.2 nozzle
* **Quality**
    * **Layer height**: Changed `Layer height` from `0.08 mm` to `0.06 mm`.
* **Strength**
    * **Walls**: Increased `Wall loops` from `4` to `6`.
    * **Top/bottom shells**: 
        * Increased `Top shell layers` from `7` to `10`.
        * Increased `Bottom shell layers` from `5` to `8`.
* **Speed**
    * **Other layers speed**: 
        * Decreased `Outer wall` speed from `60 mm/s` to `30 mm/s`.
        * Decreased `Inner wall` speed from `150 mm/s` to `80 mm/s`.
* **Support**
    * **Support**: 
        * Changed `Enable support` from `No` to `Yes`.
        * Changed `Style` from `Default (Grid)` to `Tree Slim`.
    * **Advanced**: 
        * Increased `Top Z distance` from `0.08 mm` to `0.12 mm`.
        * Increased `Bottom Z distance` from `0.08 mm` to `0.12 mm`.
        * Increased `Top interface layers` from `2 layers` to `3 layers`.
        * Increased `Bottom interface layers` from `2 layers` to `3 layers`.
